About page

This web application takes user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name



History behind Akan Names

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first names as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
